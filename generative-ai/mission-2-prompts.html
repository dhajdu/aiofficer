Mission 2 — Challenge Prompts

Every prompt is copy-paste ready. Replace anything in [brackets] with your specific information. The data is the fuel. These frameworks help you find it, fix it, and make it matter.

💡 How to use these: Copy any prompt, paste it into your AI tool, fill in the brackets with your real data. But first, check your settings. Once your data is out there, you cannot take it back.
Find the Root Cause of AI Failure in Your Organization
Diagnostic Prompt
Diagnose Your AI Program
Understand why AI programs are not delivering results at your organization
I want to understand why AI programs fail and how to prevent it at my organization. My team is [size] people in [department/industry]. We have tried [describe any AI tools or initiatives tried]. Ask me about our workflows, our data situation, and our current tool usage, then tell me the most likely root causes of our AI program problems and what an AI Officer would fix first.
Identify Your Data Moat
Strategy Prompt
Find Your Competitive Data Advantage
Identify the unique data your organization has that nobody else can replicate
I want to identify my organization's data moat. We work in [industry] and our main business activities are [describe what your team does]. Ask me about the data we collect, where it lives, and how organized it is. Then tell me: what data do we have that competitors cannot easily replicate, and what would need to happen to make it AI-ready?
Data Audit - What You Have and What It Needs
Audit Prompt
Audit Your Data Before Building
Assess whether your data is ready for an AI program
Help me audit the data for an AI program I am planning. The program goal is [describe what you want AI to do]. Before you start, ask me:
- What data sources are involved?
- Where does that data currently live?
- How consistently is it formatted?
- Are there known gaps or quality issues?
- Who owns the data and who has access?

After I answer, give me a data readiness score and tell me exactly what needs to be fixed before this program can work.
Clean a Messy Dataset
Hands-On Prompt
Fix Your Data Step by Step
Use this when you have a dataset that needs cleaning before AI can use it
I am going to paste a dataset that I need to clean before using it in an AI program. When I paste it, do the following:

1. Identify every data quality problem you find (duplicates, inconsistent formats, missing fields, wrong data types, outliers)
2. For each problem, tell me what it is and where it appears
3. Produce a cleaned version of the dataset
4. Tell me what you changed and flag anything you could not fix

Do not make up values for missing fields. Flag them instead. After cleaning, verify your own work: check the row count, check for remaining duplicates, and confirm the formats are consistent.
Verify AI's Data Work
Verification Prompt
Check AI's Output Before Trusting Numbers
Audit AI's analysis to catch errors before they reach leadership
I am going to paste a dataset and an analysis AI produced from it. Your job is to verify the analysis, not accept it. Check:
- Does the row count in the analysis match the actual dataset?
- Are there any numbers that do not add up?
- Did AI make any assumptions that are not stated?
- Are there any patterns in the data that the analysis missed or misrepresented?
- What is the single biggest risk in this analysis if it went to leadership?

Report any discrepancies directly. Do not soften the feedback.
Conduct a Settings Audit for Your Team
Governance Prompt
Check Your AI Tool's Privacy Settings
Understand how your AI tool handles your company's data
Walk me through how to check my privacy and data settings in [ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini]. Tell me:
- Where to find the settings
- What each relevant setting controls
- What the safest configuration looks like for professional work
- How to check whether my data is being used to train the model

After you explain the settings, help me determine whether the current tier I am on ([free / paid / enterprise]) is appropriate for the type of data my team works with.
Evaluate an AI Tool for a Program
Selection Prompt
Choose the Right AI for Your Program
Apply the four selection criteria to pick the best tool
I am evaluating AI tools for a program at my organization. Help me apply the four selection criteria. Before you give a recommendation, ask me:
- What is the program trying to accomplish?
- What type of data is involved?
- What tier does my organization need (free / paid / enterprise)?
- What tools does my team already use day-to-day?
- Are there specific safety or compliance requirements?

After I answer, recommend the best AI Kingdom for this program and explain why using all four criteria: data advantage, interface features, safety controls, and integration.
Build Your Organization's Data Moat - Step by Step
Planning Prompt
Create a Data Organization Action Plan
Turn your best data into an AI-ready asset
I want to build a data moat for my organization using the four-step process: Capture, Clean, Connect, Reuse. My organization works in [industry] and I want to start with the [Revenue / Talent / Operations / Innovation] office. Ask me about what data we already collect and how it is currently stored, then build me a step-by-step action plan for turning that data into an AI-ready asset.
