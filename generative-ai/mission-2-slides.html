Data Privacy Tiers

The Three Tiers

  • 1
    FreeYour data trains the model. Never use for work.
  • 2
    PaidYour data stays yours. Minimum for professional work.
  • 3
    EnterpriseYour data has a contract. For sensitive company data.

15
Be Careful

99% of AI apps are wrappers

For every Figma that is genuinely integrating AI, there are a thousand apps charging you monthly for a pretty interface built on top of the same model you already have. Know what is under the hood before your team signs up for anything.

16
Example

Choosing a Tool for a Program

Outcome: Automate monthly reporting

Data: Financial numbers, customer metrics, internal documents

Interface: Large Excel files, formatted outputs

Safety: Financial data = paid or enterprise tier

Integration: Team lives in Google Workspace? Then Gemini.

The answer is not "which AI is best." The answer is "which AI fits this program."
