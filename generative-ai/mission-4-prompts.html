V - Visual Anchor: [paste your starting image or describe it]. Confirm it is a strong anchor.

Style DNA & Brand Systems
Style DNA
Build Your Brand's Style DNA
Create the system document for consistent, on-brand visuals
I need to build a Style DNA document for [brand name], a [describe the brand and product]. This document will be used by our team to generate consistent, on-brand visuals without needing a designer in the loop.

Ask me about:
- The brand's personality (3-5 words)
- Color palette (primary and accent colors with hex codes if you have them)
- Typography style preference (bold/minimal/elegant/modern)
- Overall mood (premium/approachable/energetic/professional)
- The platforms we will be publishing on
- The four audience types we create visuals for most often
- Any specific visual elements that must always appear or always be avoided

After I answer, create a Style DNA template with two sections:
1. Fixed Elements (stays the same across everything)
2. Variable Elements (what shifts by platform and audience)

Format it as a document the team can use as a reference when generating visuals.

Tool Evaluation
Tool Evaluation
Which Visual AI Tool Fits Your Program?
Choose between LLMs, embedded, and purpose-built tools
I am choosing a visual AI tool for a program at my organization. Help me evaluate the options. Ask me:
- What types of visuals does this program need? (product shots / social media / video / illustrations / presentations)
- What is the skill level of the team who will generate these visuals?
- What tools does the team already use day-to-day?
- Are there specific quality or brand control requirements?
- What is the approximate volume of visuals per month?

After I answer, recommend whether I should use an LLM with image generation (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), an embedded tool (Canva, Adobe Firefly), or a purpose-built tool (Midjourney, Runway, Google Flow), and explain why using specific criteria from my situation.
