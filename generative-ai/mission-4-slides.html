The challenge

One good image is easy.
Eleven on-brand images is a system.

Most people type "make me a professional image" and hope for the best. That is not a system. That is a guess.

The gap: Between a generic AI image and a professional visual is the same gap you closed in Mission 3. Structure wins. Always.

05
Same model, different inputs

Bare prompt vs. 5-Element

  • Bare
    "Create an image of an energy drink."
    Generic can. Generic background. You'd never send it.
  • 5E
    All five elements defined.
    Looks professional. On-brand. Ready to ship.

The difference is not the AI. The difference is you.

Brief 3

Three types of visual AI tools

  • LLM
    ChatGPT, Claude, GeminiGood for most business visuals. Start here.
  • E
    Embedded: Canva, AdobeAI inside tools you already use.
  • P
    Purpose-Built: Midjourney, Runway, Google FlowWhen LLMs cannot deliver.

Most "new" tools are just wrappers around the same models. Know what is under the hood.
