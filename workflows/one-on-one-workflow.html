Overview

How This Workflow Runs

Every 1-1 follows the same five-step rhythm. AI handles the prep, the summary, the follow-up, and the trend analysis. You show up and coach. The system holds you to your own leadership standards so your team gets the same experience every time.

The Problem

Inconsistent 1-1s

  • Prep is ad hoc or skipped entirely
  • Coaching questions lack focus and depth
  • Commitments are made but not tracked
  • No follow-up between meetings
  • Patterns and growth trends go unnoticed

The Solution

AI-powered rhythm

  • AI prepares you using your own coaching docs
  • Meeting notes are auto-summarized with commitments
  • Mid-cycle nudges keep accountability alive
  • Monthly trend analysis surfaces patterns
  • Your leadership brand shows up consistently
One system. Consistent leadership. Your leadership brand identity kit, emotional intelligence guide, communication style guide, and coaching profile are loaded into every AI step, so the system coaches you in your own voice.

The Cycle

Biweekly Workflow at a Glance

Four steps repeat every two weeks, plus a monthly deep review. Purple steps are fully automated. You only show up for the green one.

The Process

Five Steps, Every Cycle

Follow these steps in order. Steps 1, 3, 4, and 5 are fully AI-driven. Step 2 is where you show up and lead.

1

Friday · AI Automated

AI Prep

Every Friday before a 1-1 week, AI reviews the last 1-2 weeks of notes, open commitments, OKRs, and your leadership and coaching documents. It produces a prep doc written in your coaching voice with focus areas, suggested questions, and context reminders.

  • Reviews most recent meeting summary and commitment status
  • Pulls current OKRs and flags anything off-track or approaching deadline
  • References your leadership brand identity kit, EQ guide, and communication style guide
  • Generates 3-5 coaching-aligned questions tailored to this person's current situation
  • Adds context reminders (personal situations, company context, upcoming milestones)
  • Writes the prep doc to Google Doc
2

Wednesday · Human

The 1-1 Meeting

You run the meeting. AI listens via the meeting recording. This is where coaching, accountability, and human connection happen. The prep doc gives you everything you need to show up consistently.

  • Review the AI-generated prep doc before the call
  • Use the suggested questions as a starting framework (adapt as needed)
  • Coach, listen, challenge, support
  • AI records the meeting for post-processing
3

Wednesday Post-Meeting · AI Automated

Summary and Commitments

Immediately after the 1-1 ends, AI processes the meeting recording. It writes a structured summary, extracts commitments (not tasks, commitments), and updates the Google Doc.

  • Transcribes and processes the AI meeting recording
  • Writes a concise meeting summary capturing key discussion points
  • Extracts specific commitments made by both parties (you and the direct report)
  • Flags any emotional or personal context worth tracking
  • Updates the person's Google Doc with the summary and commitment log
  • Notes any items that connect to OKRs or previous commitments
4

Following Wednesday · AI Automated

Mid-Cycle Check-in

One week after the 1-1 (on the off-week Wednesday), AI sends a check-in nudge. This keeps accountability alive between meetings and captures status updates that feed into the next prep.

  • Sends a check-in prompt referencing open commitments from the last 1-1
  • Asks for brief status updates on each commitment
  • Captures any commentary or context changes
  • Writes updates to the Google Doc for the next prep cycle
5

Monthly · AI Automated

Trend Analysis

Once a month, AI steps back and looks at the bigger picture. It reviews the full month of 1-1 data plus prior quarters to surface patterns, growth themes, recurring blockers, and coaching insights you might miss in the week-to-week.

  • Analyzes all meeting summaries and commitment logs from the past month
  • Compares against prior quarters to identify growth trajectory
  • Surfaces recurring themes: what topics keep coming up?
  • Identifies patterns in commitment follow-through
  • Highlights coaching opportunities and flags potential concerns
  • Writes a trend report to the Google Doc with actionable insights

Data Requirements

What This Workflow Needs

The AI draws from two types of data: your leadership foundation (which shapes how it coaches) and per-person data (which shapes what it focuses on). All data lives in Lark and Google Docs.

Leadership Foundation

Required

Leadership Brand Identity Kit

Defines your leadership voice, values, and how you want to show up. AI uses this to frame questions and reminders in your own style.

Required

Emotional Intelligence Guide

Your EQ framework. Informs how AI handles sensitive context like personal situations or difficult conversations.

Required

Communication Style Guide

Your preferred communication patterns. Shapes the tone, structure, and directness of AI-generated prep and questions.

Required

Coaching Profile and Instructions

How you want to coach. The rules and principles AI follows when generating coaching questions and feedback.

Per-Person Data

Per Person

Individual Profile

Background, strengths, growth areas, communication preferences. Completed for Trac and Mai.

Per Person

OKRs and Goals

Written goals for each person. Set quarterly. AI references these in every prep and trend analysis.

Ongoing

Meeting Notes

Previous 1-1 summaries and commitment logs. AI looks back 1-2 weeks for prep, full quarter for trends.

Ongoing

AI Meeting Recordings

Recordings from each 1-1. AI processes these to generate summaries and extract commitments.

Outputs

What Gets Produced Each Cycle

Every cycle generates these outputs, all written to a Google Doc per person. Eventually these will migrate to Lark.

prep-doc meeting-summary commitment-log check-in-status trend-report

Biweekly Outputs

Monthly

Trend Report
